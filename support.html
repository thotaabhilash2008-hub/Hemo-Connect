Health Screening

Eligibility Checklist

  • ✓ Age: 18 – 65 years old
  • ✓ Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • ✓ General Health: No recent illness/infection
  • ✓ Hemoglobin: ≥ 12.5 g/dL

Post-Donation Care

  • 🥤 Drink plenty of fluids
  • 🛋️ Avoid heavy exercise for 24h
  • 🩹 Keep bandage on for 4-6 hours
  • 🍎 Eat iron-rich foods

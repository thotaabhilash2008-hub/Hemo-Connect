Donor Health & Nutrition

Fuel your body to save others. Your recovery starts with the right fuel.

🥗🍎💪
📅

Pre-Donation Prep

  • ✓ Iron-Rich Diet: Focus on spinach, beans, and lean meats 48h before.
  • ✓ Hydrate: Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily.
  • ✓ Power Sleep: Ensure a full 7-9 hours of rest the night before.
  • ✓ Light Meal: Eat a non-fatty meal 2-3 hours before donating.
🩹

Recovery Steps

01. Drink an extra 4 glasses of fluids in the first 24 hours.
02. Keep the strip bandage on for the next 4-6 hours.
03. Avoid heavy lifting or intense exercise for the rest of the day.
04. If you feel dizzy, sit or lie down until you feel better.
